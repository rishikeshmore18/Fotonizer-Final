# PhotoApp10 Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: September 25, 2025**

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Photos and Media
- **Local Storage**: All photos are stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Backup**: Photos may be backed up to Google Drive (with your explicit consent)
- **Metadata**: We collect photo metadata (size, date, location if available)

### 1.2 Usage Analytics
- **App Usage**: We track how you use the app to improve performance
- **Crash Reports**: We collect crash reports to fix bugs
- **Performance Data**: We monitor app performance to optimize user experience

### 1.3 Device Information
- **Device Model**: To provide device-specific optimizations
- **Android Version**: To ensure compatibility
- **App Version**: To provide appropriate features

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Photo Management
- Store and organize your photos locally
- Generate thumbnails for faster browsing
- Provide search and filtering capabilities

### 2.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)
- Backup photos to Google Drive (only with your consent)
- Sync photos across devices
- Restore photos if needed

### 2.3 App Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Fix bugs and crashes
- Optimize performance

## 3. Data Security

### 3.1 Local Security
- All photos are encrypted locally
- Secure storage using Android's security features
- No unauthorized access to your photos

### 3.2 Cloud Security
- Google Drive integration uses OAuth2 authentication
- All cloud communications are encrypted
- We don't store your Google credentials

### 3.3 Privacy Protection
- No personal data is shared with third parties
- Analytics data is anonymized
- You can opt-out of analytics at any time

## 4. Your Rights

### 4.1 Data Control
- Delete photos at any time
- Disable cloud backup
- Opt-out of analytics
- Export your data

### 4.2 Access and Correction
- View all your stored photos
- Correct photo metadata
- Update app preferences

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Local Data
- Photos remain on your device until you delete them
- App data is retained until app uninstallation

### 5.2 Cloud Data
- Cloud backups remain until you delete them
- We don't retain deleted photos

### 5.3 Analytics Data
- Usage analytics are retained for 2 years
- Crash reports are retained for 1 year
- All data is anonymized

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 6.1 Google Services
- **Google Drive**: For cloud backup (optional)
- **Google Analytics**: For app usage analytics
- **Firebase**: For crash reporting and performance monitoring

### 6.2 Data Sharing
- We don't sell your data
- We don't share personal information
- Third-party services have their own privacy policies

## 7. Children's Privacy

- We don't knowingly collect data from children
- Parents should supervise children's app usage
