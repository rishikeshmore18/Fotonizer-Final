Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. Foto-Nizer stores all your photos and videos in app-private storage on your device. We use Google Drive for backup, but we do not collect, store, or access any of your personal data.

  • ✅ All data stored locally on your device
  • ✅ Google Drive backup is optional and user-controlled
  • ✅ No data collection or analytics
  • ✅ No third-party tracking
  • ✅ Account isolation ensures data privacy
